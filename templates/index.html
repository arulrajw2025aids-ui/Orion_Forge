OUR PROCESS

How It Works

From drone deployment to active rescue, our automated system ensures minimal delay.

I
UAV Deployment
Drones are deployed over disaster-struck areas to continuously capture live video feeds.
II
AI Processing
The YOLOv8 AI engine scans incoming footage for human shapes and assesses severity in real-time.
III
Instant Alerts
If a human is detected, GPS coordinates are logged, and an alert is broadcasted immediately.
IV
Rescue Action
Rescue teams receive SMS alerts with coordinates, allowing for swift, targeted operations.
